Agatha Corcoran

Make an appointment Close

Agatha Corcoran, BSW, MSW, RSW is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker who has been operating her own private practice (Atlantic Counselling) for the past 14 years. She graduated from the University of Toronto with a Master of Social Work Degree specializing in Addictions and Mental Health. Prior to that she earned a Bachelor of Social Work Degree and a Certificate of Criminology from Memorial University of Newfoundland.

Agatha specializes in Alcohol and Drug Dependency and works continually with clients diagnosed with Post Traumatic Stress Disorder who have been deeply affected by domestic violence/abuse/trauma. She also provides intervention to clients struggling with grief/loss, depression and anxiety. She is a Certified Responder in Critical Incident Stress Management for Individuals and Groups and has extensive experience in Crisis Intervention and conducting Suicide Risk Assessments.

Agatha specializes in Individual Therapy in the areas of Addictions, PTSD, Grief/Loss, Adult Survivors of Childhood Sexual Abuse, Domestic Violence and Trauma/Abuse.

Carolyn specializes in Individual Therapy in the areas of Mental Health including Anxiety, Depression, Borderline Personality Disorder and Trauma/Abuse. She also provides intervention in the areas of Grief and Loss, Separation/Divorce and Life Transition Issues.

Jim Russell

Make an appointment Close

Jim Russell, BA, BEd, M.Div, MEd (Counselling), CCC is a Canadian Certified Counsellor. He graduated from Acadia University with a Master of Education(Counselling), and a Master of Divinity from the Atlantic School of Theology.

Jim has over 21 years experience including 5 years with the Correctional Service of Canada, 11 years within the Canadian Armed Forces and 4 years with Youth Corrections.

His approach draws on a variety of counselling styles that are primarily person centered. He focuses on building rapport, trust and a strong therapeutic relationship as he supports people through life's difficult crises and transitions. Jim's approach is Solution Focused and Strength Based whereby strengths, resources and solutions are identified and strategies developed together to achieve personal growth and the transformation of problematic thinking.

Jim has worked extensively with clients who are in need of Crisis Intervention, Grief Counselling, Suicide Intervention, and those struggling with Anxiety, Depression, Relationship Difficulties, Divorce, Job Loss and Chronic Illness.

She graduated from Trinity Western University with a Master of Arts in Counselling Psychology. She also earned a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from Crandall University.
